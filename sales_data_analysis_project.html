Project Overview¶

This project analyzes a real-world e-commerce dataset to uncover trends in sales performance. we aim to answer key business questions such as:

  • What was the best month for sales?
  • Which city had the highest number of sales?
  • at what time are customers most likely to purchase?
  • Which products sold the most and why?

we use Python libraries such as Pandas, Matplotlib to perform the analysis and create visual insigths.

Conclusion¶

  • December had the highest total sales, suggesting strong holiday shopping activity.
  • San Francisco led in overall revenue, likely due to high population and spending.
  • Most purchases occurred around 11 AM and 7 PM — optimal for marketing campaigns.
  • AAA Batteries (4-pack) and USB-C Charging Cables were the most sold items — likely due to low price and high utility.

These insights can guide better inventory and advertising strategies.
